About Me

I am a local teacher and parent with a background in psychology, data and mental health support. I’m running for council to build a safer, more resilient community.

With a history of volunteering for community organisations and working with young people, I aim to develop a whānau safety network, providing positive alternatives for at-risk rangatahi.

Through the Samaritans and local men’s groups, I’ve seen the need for community wellbeing. My drop-in centre supports this with free gym sessions and a comfortable space to connect and learn.
